About

My career started with my love for logic and research.
I have worked as an investigator, a researcher, an intelligence analyst, a Systems Analyst and a Project Manager.
I've learned to listen, to inquire, to organize, and to produce.
My focus has always been listening to individuals and their unique stories so that I may understand their purpose and goals.
I have pursued learning opportunities all along my journey to achieve a BS, MBA, and PMP.
My desire to work with people, coupled with my love of technology, has led me to become a software developer.
For me, it's the perfect combination of listening to users, logical organization, and implementing technology to assist in reaching goals.
I look forward to bringing my passion of working with users to help find answers and deliver results in your organization.
